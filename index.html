About

As a long time tech enthusiast I have always found myself researching new technological advancements, watching keynotes from notable leaders in the tech industry, as well as a passion for understanding and troubleshooting hardware and software.awesome work.

I am first and foremost a student who is willing to learn and obtain new skills to become a balanced intellect. I come from a small town in Southern New Jersey where I grew up as a first-generation Indian American where diversity was scarce. Fighting through all of my insecurities, challenges, and hardships that came from being one of the only Indian kids in my town I went through high school with top marks and glowing recommendations from my instructors. With the drive to become a doctor, I started my journey at New York University studying Biomedical Sciences at the engineering school, however after working in a hospital for almost a year I realized that a hospital was not the right environment for me and I began finding my true passions. After a few years of studying psychology, finding my passion for photography, and rediscovering my love for all things technology I graduated from NYU in 2020 ready to meet the world of opportunity, however, due to the raging pandemic jobs in my field were scarce. Due to this, I decided to strengthen my skills in technology and started teaching myself languages such as Javascript, HTML, and Python. Soon after I applied to App Academy where I spent the past few months sharpening my skills in Javascript, Python, React/Redux, HTML, CSS, and Database manipulation. I have spent 1000+ hours pair programming and working with different teams to work on practice problems as well as building out full stack web applications in a weeks time. I am eager to build upon the skills that I have acquired at App Academy and am pursuing a software engineering role where I can grow and learn with the help of a mentor as well as contribute to my team and the overall success of the company. Feel free to reach out to me at anytime I am willing to talk shop or just to have a chat.
